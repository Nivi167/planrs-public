Arrange the Skittles and pour warm water! 💧
💡 What is Dissolving?

When warm water touches the Skittles, the coloured sugar coating dissolves — it melts into the water! The colour then travels inward toward the centre of the plate, making a beautiful rainbow. Just like sugar disappears in nimbu paani 🍋, the candy coating disappears into the water!
