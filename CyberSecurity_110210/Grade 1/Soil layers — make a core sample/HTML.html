Layer Properties

Humus: Dark, soft, rich in nutrients. Plants and worms live here.
Topsoil: Brown, crumbly. Where most roots grow.
Subsoil: Lighter, more clay. Fewer organisms.
Parent Rock: Broken rock fragments. Becoming soil slowly.
Bedrock: Solid rock. Cannot be broken by hand.
Remember: Surface layers are richest in life. Deepest layers are oldest rock!
