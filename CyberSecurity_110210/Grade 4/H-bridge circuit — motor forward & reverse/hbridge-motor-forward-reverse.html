WELCOME TO THE CIRCUIT LAB
A motor is just electricity flowing in one direction. Want it to spin backwards? Simply flip the flow. That's the magic of the H-Bridge!
💡 Think of it like this: A 4-way junction on an Indian road — open the right gates and your car (electricity) moves exactly where you want it.
✅
CIRCUIT ASSEMBLED!
All components are connected. The H-Bridge is built — 4 switches form the "H" shape, with the motor in the middle crossbar.
🔌 Why the letter H? Draw the circuit — two vertical lines (the arms), one horizontal line (the motor). It literally looks like the letter H!
🔄
DIRECTION MASTER!
You've discovered the secret: A1+B2 = Forward (Seedha) and A2+B1 = Reverse (Ulta). The electricity literally flows the other way!
🚗 Power Windows: In your Maruti or Tata car, pressing UP vs DOWN simply changes which switch pair is active — exactly what you just did!
🏆
CIRCUIT CAPTAIN — CERTIFIED!
You can build, control, and apply H-Bridge circuits to real machines. Remote cars, washing machines, power windows — you now understand how they all work!
⚡ Big Picture: Engineers use the same H-Bridge principle in robots, electric vehicles, and even the motors inside your computer's cooling fans — just at a much smaller scale!
