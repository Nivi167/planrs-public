What is a Variable?

A variable is a mystery number we use a letter for — like x. It's the number we need to find!

⚖️

What is an Equation?

An equation says two things are EQUAL. Whatever you do to one side, you MUST do to the other!

1️⃣2️⃣

Two-Step Equations

These need two moves to find x — undo addition/subtraction first, then multiply/divide!

🔄

Inverse Operations

The reverse of + is −, and the reverse of × is ÷. We use these to isolate x!

📌 Example: Solve 2x + 3 = 11

Start2x + 3 = 11
→
Step 1: −32x = 8
→
Step 2: ÷2x = 4

First undo +3 by subtracting 3 from both sides, then divide both sides by 2!
