What is Inflation?

Discover why things cost more every year — and what it means for your money!

📈

What is Inflation?

Inflation is when prices slowly rise over time — so the same ₹10 buys less than it did before!

🏦

Why Does It Happen?

When more money chases fewer goods, sellers raise prices. More demand or higher costs push prices up.

📊

CPI — Price Index

Governments track a "basket" of everyday items to measure how much prices have risen. That's the CPI!

💡

Purchasing Power

If prices rise but your allowance stays the same, you can buy fewer things. That's less purchasing power!
