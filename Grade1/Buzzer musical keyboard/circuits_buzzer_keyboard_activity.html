🇮🇳 In India, aluminium foil (like the kind used to pack food at weddings!) is an excellent conductor — that's why it works as the "keys" on a DIY buzzer keyboard! Plastic wrap is an insulator — it blocks electricity. Smart materials make smart circuits!
