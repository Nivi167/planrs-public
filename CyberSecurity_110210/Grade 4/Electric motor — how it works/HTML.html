⚡ HOW IT WORKS
🔄 FLEMINGS RULE Current in a magnetic field creates a force — and that force causes ROTATION!
〰 CURRENTElectricity flows through the coil, making it an electromagnet.

⚡ FUN FACTS
🌀 HOMOPOLAR MOTOR The simplest electric motor ever! Invented in 1821 by Michael Faraday using just a wire, battery & magnet.
💜 NEO MAGNETSuper-strong neodymium magnet sticks to battery base and creates the field.
🔋 AA BATTERYStands upright — current flows from positive tip down through the wire.
🔶 COPPER WIRETouches both battery tip and magnet edge — it spins due to the Lorentz force!
