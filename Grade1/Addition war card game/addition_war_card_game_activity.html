💡 How to Play

Both players flip cards and add the numbers together to get their SUM. The player with the BIGGER SUM wins that round! In Round 1 flip 2 cards, in Round 2 flip 3 cards — add them all up! Like counting cricket runs: 4 runs + 6 runs = 10 runs total!
