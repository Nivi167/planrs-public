🪄 How Weights Work
Imagine weights like volume knobs on a magical radio 📻. A high weight means "LISTEN to this signal!". A low weight means "ignore it". The network multiplies each input by its weight and adds them up. That's how it decides what it sees! 🧙‍♂️
