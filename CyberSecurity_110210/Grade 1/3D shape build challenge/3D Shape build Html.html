📋 How to Build
  • 1
    Drag 8 marshmallows onto the build area — these are the corners!
  • 2
    Switch to Connect mode (tab above).
  • 3
    Click one marshmallow, then click another to draw a stick between them.
  • 4
    Add 12 sticks total to form all the edges of your cube.
  • 5
    Press "Check My Cube!" when you think you're done!
Use this as a guide! Your cube doesn't have to look exactly like this — just make sure you have 8 corners and 12 edges connected!
