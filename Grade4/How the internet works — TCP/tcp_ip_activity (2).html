📦 Mission: Build a Data Packet

Every message on the internet is broken into packets. Each packet has three parts: a Header (address info), a Payload (actual data), and a Trailer (error check). Drag the correct pieces into each slot!

🗺️ Mission: Find the Best Route

Routers choose the fastest path for packets. Some paths are blocked 🚫. Your job: choose the correct routing method for each scenario, then trace the best path!

🤝 Mission: Complete the TCP Handshake

Before two computers send data, they do a 3-Step Handshake. Click the messages in the correct order to establish a connection!
