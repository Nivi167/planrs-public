Welcome, Sound Scientist!
Did you know? Every sound you've ever heard was made by something shaking!

That shaking is called VIBRATION.
Vibration makes sound. When vibration stops — sound stops too!
🥁 When Riya beats her dholak during Pongal, the drum skin vibrates — that's what makes the amazing sound!
🎸
Amazing! You heard it!
When you plucked the rubber band, it vibrated (shook really fast) and made a sound!

When you stopped the shaking — the sound disappeared too!
🎵 A Sitar or Veena works the same way — pluck the string, it vibrates, you hear music!
VIBRATION = कंपन (Kampan)
