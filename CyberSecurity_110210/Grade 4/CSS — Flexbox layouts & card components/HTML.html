ALIGNMENT MASTERED!
You now control both axes! justify-content handles horizontal spread; align-items handles vertical positioning.
✅ justify-content: center → Centres items on main axis
✅ justify-content: space-between → Even gaps between items
✅ align-items: flex-start → Items sit at the top of container
