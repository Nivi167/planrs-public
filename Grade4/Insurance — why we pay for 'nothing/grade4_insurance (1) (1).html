What is Insurance?

A promise to help pay for big unexpected costs — like a safety net for life's surprises!

💰

Premium

The regular amount you pay to keep your insurance active — like a subscription for protection.

📋

Policy

The written agreement between you and the insurance company that explains what's covered.

📣

Claim

When something bad happens, you ask your insurance company for help — that's a claim!
