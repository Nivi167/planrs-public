🔑 Password Tips — Remember These!

✅ Strong passwords have: 8+ characters, UPPERCASE, lowercase, numbers & symbols (!@#)
❌ Weak passwords use: your name, birthday, or simple patterns like 123456
💡 Trick: Use a phrase! "I love chai" → ILov3Ch@i!
🔒 Never share your password with ANYONE — even your best friend
🔄 Change your password every 3–6 months for extra safety
